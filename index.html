Game Objective.
Help Casey knock down as many fruits as you can in the allotted time.

Each fruit has a different point value:
 - **Cherries**: 5 points
- **Oranges**: 3 points
- **Watermelons**: 1 point

Level Duration
The time allotted depends on the difficulty level you choose:
- Difficult**: 30 seconds
- Normal**: 1 minute
- Easy**: 2 minutes

How to Play:
1. Choose your difficulty level.
2. Use the arrow keys to move Casey's bow sight.
3. Press the space key to shoot.
4. Try to get the highest score possible before the time runs out!
